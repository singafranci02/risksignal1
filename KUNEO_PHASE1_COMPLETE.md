## ðŸ’¡ Key Insights

### What Worked Well
1. **Clear Positioning**: The shift from "monitoring" to "infrastructure" is powerful
2. **The Digital Helmet**: Strong metaphor that resonates
3. **Three Pillars**: Easy to understand framework
4. **Regulatory Focus**: Differentiates from competitors
5. **Gradient Enhancement**: Adds visual interest without breaking monochrome theme

### Lessons Learned
1. **Gradients Need Balance**: Too many can be overwhelming
2. **Content Hierarchy**: Important to guide users through narrative
3. **Technical + Business**: Need to appeal to both audiences
4. **Compliance is Complex**: Need to simplify without losing accuracy
