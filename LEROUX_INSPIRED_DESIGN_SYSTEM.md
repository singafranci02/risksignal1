- **Border**: `#e2e8f0` - Subtle borders

- **Text Secondary**: `#4a5568` - Medium gray for body text
- **Text Muted**: `#718096` - Light gray for metadata

### Accent Colors
- **Success**: `#48bb78` - Green for positive actions
- **Warning**: `#ed8936` - Orange for attention
- **Error**: `#f56565` - Red for critical items
- **Info**: `#4299e1` - Blue for information

## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings**: Inter (700, 600, 500)
- **Body**: Inter (400, 500)
- **Monospace**: JetBrains Mono (for code)

### Scale
- **Hero**: 3.5rem - 4.5rem (56px - 72px)
- **H1**: 2.5rem - 3rem (40px - 48px)
- **H2**: 2rem - 2.25rem (32px - 36px)
- **H3**: 1.5rem - 1.75rem (24px - 28px)
- **Body**: 1rem - 1.125rem (16px - 18px)
- **Small**: 0.875rem (14px)

## Layout Principles

### Spacing
- **Section Padding**: 80px - 120px vertical
- **Card Padding**: 32px - 48px
- **Grid Gaps**: 24px - 32px
- **Max Width**: 1280px (content)

### Grid System
- **Desktop**: 12-column grid
- **Tablet**: 8-column grid
- **Mobile**: 4-column grid

## Component Patterns

### Hero Sections
- Full-width background images
- Overlay gradients (navy to transparent)
- Large, bold headlines
- Clear CTAs
- Trust indicators below fold

### Feature Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Icon at top (colored)
- Heading + description
- Hover: Lift effect (shadow increase)

### Image Sections
- Large, high-quality photos
- 50/50 image-text splits
- Parallax effects on scroll
- Professional business imagery

### Stats/Numbers
- Large numbers (48px+)
- Small labels below
- Grouped in 3-4 columns
- Light background sections

### Testimonials
- Quote marks
- Client photo
- Name + company
- Carousel layout

### CTAs
- Full-width sections
- Colored backgrounds
- White text
- Large buttons

## Image Strategy

### Hero Images
- Professional office/business settings
- Team collaboration photos
- Technology/digital imagery
- High-resolution (1920x1080+)

### Feature Images
- Icons: Custom illustrated icons
- Photos: Professional stock photos
- Diagrams: Clean, minimal infographics

### Recommended Sources
- Unsplash (free, high-quality)
- Pexels (free business photos)
- Custom illustrations

---

## Animation Principles

### Subtle Interactions
- Hover: Scale 1.02, shadow increase
- Transitions: 200-300ms ease
- Scroll: Fade-in on viewport entry
- Parallax: Slow, smooth movement

### No Excessive Motion
- Avoid spinning
- Avoid bouncing
- Avoid sliding
- Keep it professional
