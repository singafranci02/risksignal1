## üé® New Color Scheme

### Why the Change?
Pure black (`#000000`) was causing eye strain and poor readability. The new slate-based palette provides:
- Better contrast for text
- Easier on eyes (blue undertones)
- Professional institutional feel
- Clear visual hierarchy

### Color Palette

**Backgrounds:**
- Primary: `bg-slate-950` (deep blue-black)
- Surface: `bg-slate-900` (cards)
- Surface Variant: `bg-slate-800` (hover states)
- Elevated: `bg-slate-950` (code blocks)

**Text:**
- Primary: `text-slate-100` (main content)
- Secondary: `text-slate-400` (supporting text)
- Muted: `text-slate-500` (metadata)

**Accents:**
- Primary: `blue-500` (trust, technology)
- Secondary: `indigo-500` (governance)
- Success: `emerald-500` (compliance)
- Warning: `amber-500` (attention)
- Danger: `red-500` (critical)

**Borders:**
- Default: `border-slate-800`
- Hover: `border-slate-700`
- Active: `border-blue-500`

**Gradients:**
- Buttons: `from-blue-500 to-indigo-600`
- Cards: `from-slate-900 via-slate-900 to-slate-800`
- Highlights: `from-blue-500/10 via-indigo-500/10 to-purple-500/10`
