## New Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: `bg-slate-950` (deep blue-black, easier on eyes)
- **Surface**: `bg-slate-900` (cards, elevated elements)
- **Surface Variant**: `bg-slate-800` (hover states)

### Accent Colors
- **Primary**: `blue-500` (trust, technology)
- **Secondary**: `indigo-500` (governance, authority)
- **Success**: `emerald-500` (compliance, verified)
- **Warning**: `amber-500` (alerts, attention)
- **Danger**: `red-500` (violations, critical)

### Text Colors
- **Primary**: `text-slate-100` (main content)
- **Secondary**: `text-slate-400` (supporting text)
- **Muted**: `text-slate-500` (metadata, labels)

### Borders
- **Default**: `border-slate-800`
- **Hover**: `border-slate-700`
- **Active**: `border-blue-500`

### Gradients
- **Hero**: `from-blue-600 via-indigo-600 to-purple-600`
- **Cards**: `from-slate-900 via-slate-800 to-slate-900`
- **Buttons**: `from-blue-500 to-indigo-600`

## Rationale

1. **Readability**: Slate tones have blue undertones, easier on eyes than pure black
2. **Hierarchy**: Clear distinction between background, surface, and content
3. **Brand**: Blue/indigo conveys trust, technology, and governance
4. **Accessibility**: Better contrast ratios for text
5. **Professional**: Maintains institutional feel without being harsh
